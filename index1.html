Scripture Of The Moon Elf(Passive Skill)
Code of Moon Elves will charge energy into Estes slowly. When it stack up to 100, it will improve his next Basic Attack, dealing 250 (+100%Total Physical Attack) (+150%Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to the target while slowing it down. The damage will ricochet, dealing 125 (+50%Total Physical Attack)(+75%Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to nearby enemies and slowing them by 60%. Lasts 1.5s.
Smart Heart (Passive Skill)
Each time Angela uses a skill, her movement speed increases by 10% for 4s, up to 30%. This acceleration effect will also be applied to her attached ally.
Noumenon Energy Core(Passive Skill)
If Lolita takes no damage in 5 seconds, Noumenon Energy Core will provide a shield that can take 300(+40*Hero Level) points of damage, lasting 20s. Lolita's nearby allies can get shields while approaching her.
